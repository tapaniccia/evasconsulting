The Founder Bottleneck Assessment

Can your business run without you? A 20-question self-assessment to find out.

Why this matters: Most founders don't burn out because they lack drive. They burn out because their business was never built to run without them. Every approval that routes through you, every question your team could answer themselves but doesn't, every task only you know how to do — these are bottlenecks. And bottlenecks don't go away as you grow. They get worse. This takes about 10 minutes. Answer honestly, based on how things actually work today — not how you wish they worked. A higher score means more founder dependency, not less — this measures friction, not health.
